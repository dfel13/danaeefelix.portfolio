About Me
Senior at the University of California, Davis. I love GIS as it is a combination of my two interests: geography and technology. It is amazing to see how advanced technology can help us pinpoint issues and capable of predicting future problems, thus allowing discussions ahead of time for how to face problems. GIS is the future!
I am an analytical and ambitious person with a passion for geospatial analytics and mapping. I am an independent thinker with zero fear of being exposed to new ways of thinking. I know how to manage my time and quickly do problem-solving. Working as a GIS Student intern at the UC Davis DataLab, I gained experience working on a multidisciplinary team in which every member had different expertise. My time at the internship gave me a chance to not only improve my analytical skills, but also my interpersonal skills. Moreover, I learned much about what it takes to be a part of a solid team and be reliable enough to complete independent work.
