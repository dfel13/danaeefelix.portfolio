GPS Befriends GIS
This project consisted of learning more calculation methods and the unique functionality of a GPS.
Project Details
This is a semivariogram map of soil transects. The radiu of influence is 5.16m and the randome error is 0. Through analysis, it can be seen that with regards to soil conductivity, the soil particles are more impacted by particles within the 5.16m range.
In addition, this map indicates that the closer the cursor gets to the center of the radius of influence(the 5.16m range) the more weight the particle has.
As can be seen, the RMSE for particular semivariogram map was 0.6551844583708. For this same map I changed the IDW method to the Kriging method and the RMSE was 1.14449821702854. Thus, if I had to choose which method is overall better and creates a smoother surface, it would be the Kriging method as its error is smaller than the IDW method.
