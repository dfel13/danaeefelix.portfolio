Boolean Rasters
Let's analyze the vegetation and road density of the NFS boundary!
Project Details / Background
This is a vegetation boolean raster performed on an NFS boundary. Producing a boolean raster helps us focus on a specific aspect of a site that we wish to analyze, in this case we focus on the vegetation density.
C/B raster cells have the value of 1, thus being true. Meanwhile, a 0/A raster cell is either untrue or there was no data found regarding vegetation. Furthermore, this project concludes there to be low vegetation density in the NFS boundary.
Road Density in the NFS Boundary
Additionally, I produced a road buffer Boolean raster of the NFS Boundary for further analysis of the amount space being utilized for transportation.
